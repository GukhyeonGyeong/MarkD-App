# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 10, 2026**

Eyaa Moment built the MarkD app as a free app. This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information when you use our app.

## Information Collection and Use

MarkD does not collect any personally identifiable information. All documents and data are stored locally on your device. No data is transmitted to external servers.

## Local Storage

All documents you save or import are stored locally on your device using iOS App Group storage, shared only between the MarkD app and its Share Extension. This data never leaves your device.

## Share Extension

When you use the Share Extension to save content from other apps, that content is saved directly to your device. It is not sent to any external server or third party.

## Third-Party Services

MarkD does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

MarkD does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
